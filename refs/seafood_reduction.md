# Seafood Reduction

## Ingredients

1. onion
2. butternut squash
3. sweet potato
4. fish stock
5. salmon
6. prawns
7. limes, dill, cilantro, chilli (optional)

## Instructions

1. prep: cut onion, cut sweet potat, butternut squash
2. prep: cut salmons and salt shrimps
3. fry up onion, add in starches and cook until soft
4. add in fish stock
5. then add chopped salmon and prawns
6. serve w lime, dill, cilantro etc
